About Ungated

Ungated is a groundbreaking group art exhibition that provides a platform for works often excluded from traditional galleries due to their use of AI or exploration of controversial themes including religious expression, political commentary, and ideological inquiry.

We believe art should spark dialogue, challenge assumptions, and push boundaries‚Äînot be silenced by gatekeeping.

  • Space: 1,500 sq ft gallery
  • Events: Panel discussion + guided tour
  • Goal: 300+ attendees
